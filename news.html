Minecraft News March 31, 2026

26.1 is here!

Minecraft has updated to 26.1 "Tiny Takeover". The server will not update until Paper and our plugins are ready. This can take anywhere from a few days to multiple weeks depending on compatibility updates.

For the full game changelog, visit the official update article: Minecraft Java Edition 26.1.

Server compatibility status

  • Core server software: waiting for stable Paper build.
  • Essential plugins: update testing in progress.
  • Public rollout: announced after full test pass.
Announcement March 24, 2026

Ranks System Update

We've updated how ranks work to better reflect activity, contribution, and community involvement rather than only time played. Promotions are no longer automatic or purely time-based.

What changed

  • More weight on positive community impact and collaboration.
  • Staff review is now part of rank advancement.
