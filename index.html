Server Rules

These sample rules are here to demonstrate the layout. Replace them with your full official rules set.

Community Standards

  • Must be 18+ to join.
  • No cracked or hacked clients are allowed.
  • No spamming, begging, or advertising.
  • Be resepectful, no harassment.
  • You must log in at least once every 30 days to be considered active.

Gameplay Rules

  • No cheating, x-ray, duping, or unfair mods.
  • No griefing, stealing, or unwanted destruction.
  • No random PVP
  • No alt-accounts.

Server Etiquette

  • Limit lag inducing activities / no chunk loaders.
  • Keep public chat in English
  • No gambling, no scamming, no illegal content/activity
  • No mini-moderating / do not try to bypass punishments.
